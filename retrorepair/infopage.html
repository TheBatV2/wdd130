NES or Nintendo Entertainment System, was Nintendos humble begining. This is where you would find games such as: Super Mario Bros 1-3, Punch out, Duck Hunt, The Legend of Zelda, Megaman, Tetris and Kirbys Adventure.

The N64 or Nintendo 64, was where Nintendo stepped into the 3D game. Here you find game such as: Super Mario 64, Golden Eye 007, Banjo-Kazooie, MarioKart 64, The Legend of Zelda:Ocarina of Time, Star Fox 64 and Diddy Kong Racing.

The GameBoy Was when Nintendo Stepped into the Handheld system, Boasting games you could play anywhere. Here you find games such as: Pokemon: Red/Blue/Yellow, The Legend of Zelda: Links Awakening, Tetris, Wario Land, Donkey Kong and Kirbys Dream Land.

The GameBoy Color is where color was finaly introduced. Innovating the handheld world again. Here you find games such as: Pokemon: The Trading Card Game, Pokemon Gold/Silver/Crystal, Donkey Kong Country and Super Mario Bros. Deluxe.

The Gameboy Advanced is where Nintendo stepped up their game with the Handhelds, This is a classic for any collection. Here you find games such as: Golden Sun, Mario and Luigi: Superstars, Metroid Fusion, The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past, Fire Emblem, Pokemon Ruby/Sapphire/Emerald/Fire Red/Leaf Green, and Castlevania:Aria of Sorrow.

The GameCube is the next non-handheld that Nintendo did. Here you find games such as: The Legend of Zelda:Windwaker/Twilight Princess, Metroid Prime series, Paper Mario: The Thousand Year Door, Super Smash Bros. Melee, Super Mario Sunshine, and Luigi's Mansion.

The DS family is where Nintendo jumped right back into the handheld game. Featuring Dual Screens (hence the name DS) one being a touchpad, brought new versitility to the console. Here you find games such as: Chrono Trigger, New Super Mario Bros. Pokemon Black/White, Animal Crossing: Wild World and Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow.

The Wii and Wii U made huge leaps as a group console, Rarely did you play alone here. Here you find games such as: Just Dance 3, Super Mario Galaxy, Wii Sports, Mario Kart Wii, Mario Party 8 and The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword.

The Nintendo Switch brought the two markets that Nintendo was working (handheld and console) and made them one, easy to add to a tv but can just be used as a handheld. Here you find games such as: Super Mario RPG: Remastered, Kirby and the Forgotten Land, Super Mario Bros. Wonder, Mario Kart 8 Deluxe, The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild/Tears of the Kingdom, and Super Smash Bros Ultimate.

The Xbox greated some of the best FPS games known to man. Here you find games such as:Halo and Halo 2, Star Wars Knights of the Old Republic, Morrowind, and Fable.

The Xbox 360 Was great at bringing people together. Here you find games such as: Bioshock, Halo 3, Skyrim, Grand Theft Auto V, Fallout 3, and Red Dead Redemption.

The Xbox 1 is a great machine to start, still in service having an open software store is a great perk. Here you find games such as: Elden Ring, Hogwarts Legacy, Assassin's Creed Valhalla, Red Dead Redemption 2, Forza Horizon 5, and MineCraft.

The Xbox X is the latest console, still in service. Here you find games such as: Lies of P, Starwars Outlaws, Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 and Cyberpunk 2077.

The Playstation 1 the Humble beginings of Sonys foray into Gaming. Here you find games such as: Crash Bandicoot, Metal Gear solid, Final Fantasy VII, Gran Turismo 2, Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2, Chrono Cross, Jack and Daxter and Spyro the Dragon.

The Playstation 2 is a nostologic classic, many peoples favorite console. Here you find games such as: Grand Theft Auto: San Andreus, Metal Grea Solid 2: Sons of Liberty, Kingdom Hearts, Devil May Cry, Rachet and Clank, and God of War.

The Playstation 3 Depending on the model could be backwards-compatable (meaning can play previous console games) making this one a favorite for those needing to save some space. Here you find games such as: Portal 2, Batman: Arkham City, Red Dead Redemption, Dark Souls, Infamous and The Last of Us.

The Playstation 4 came out at the same time as the Xbox 1, still in service, There are many great storied games on this console. Here you find games such as: Marvel's Spider Man, Ghost of Tsushima, Bloodborne, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, and Persona 5 Royal.

The Playstation 5 At this time is the fastest console on the market. Here you find games such as: God of War Ragnarok, Gran Turismo 7, Baulders Gate 3, The Last of us Part 1, and Star Wars Jedi Survivor.

The Playstation Portableis Sony's way of getting into the handheld race. Here you find games such as: Tekken 5: Dark Resurrection, Burnout Legends, Daxter, and Secret angent Clang.
